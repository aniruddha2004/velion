Privacy Policy for Personal News Assistant

Last updated: April 26, 2026

Introduction

Personal News Assistant ("the App") is a personal use application that allows you to save and organize news links locally on your device. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your data.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No third-party services are used for data collection

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using the Hive database. This includes:

  • Saved news article links
  • Article metadata (title, description, image URL) fetched temporarily for preview display

No data leaves your device at any time.

Network Usage

The App makes network requests only to:

  • Fetch article preview metadata (title, description, image) when you add a link — this is a one-time request to the article's website
  • Open links in your browser when you tap on an article

These requests are made directly from your device. No data is routed through any intermediary server.

Permissions

The App requires the following permissions:

  • Internet access — to fetch article preview metadata
  • Receive share intent — to receive shared links from other apps

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children and does not collect any data from anyone, including children.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data by:

  • Clearing the app data in your device settings
  • Uninstalling the app

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you may contact the developer through the Google Play Store listing.
